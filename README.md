# Strategic-Product-Placement-Analysis-LTVIP2026TMIDS86526
Strategic Product Placement Analysis – Sales Impact Dashboard is developed using Tableau to analyze sales performance and product placement effectiveness. The dashboard uses the Sample Superstore dataset to visualize category-wise sales, region-wise performance, monthly sales trends, top 10 revenue-generating products, and the relationship between sales and profit. The objective of this project is to identify which products and regions contribute the most to revenue and to understand seasonal sales patterns.
From the analysis, it is observed that the Technology category and West region generate higher sales compared to others. The monthly trend shows increasing sales toward the final months of the year, indicating seasonal demand. The scatter plot highlights products with high sales but low profit, helping in identifying pricing or discount issues. Overall, the dashboard provides clear insights that support strategic business decision-making and improve product placement strategies.
